Some interesting Facts about The Atlantic Trrumpet


  1. Trumpetfish are named for their long, tubular snouts that resemble a trumpet.


  2. These fish can grow up to three feet long, making them one of the larger reef fish species.


  3. Atlantic Trumpetfish have been observed swimming in pairs, with one fish acting as a lookout while the other hunts for prey.


  4. The Atlantic Trumpetfish is found in the tropical and subtropical waters of the Western Atlantic, from the Gulf of Maine to Brazil.
